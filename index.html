Our Story

Building the Future of
Ergonomic Workspaces

Founded in 2022, AdjusTables is on a mission to make ergonomic furniture accessible, affordable, and designed around your wellness needs.

Origin

Our Story

AdjusTables was born from a simple observation: traditional furniture companies were pricing ergonomic solutions out of reach for everyday workers, while quality and customization suffered through layers of retail markup.

As ergonomics experts and manufacturing innovators, AJ and Vian knew there had to be a better way. By combining direct manufacturing with cutting-edge technology like AR visualization and community-driven design, they created a new model for workplace furniture.

Today, AdjusTables serves thousands of home offices and businesses nationwide, proving that you don't have to choose between quality, affordability, and wellness.
